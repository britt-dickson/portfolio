Deeper Dive

I am a graphic design student at BCIT with a growing focus on web design and front-end development.I am interested in how design and code work together to shape clear, engaging digital experiences.

My approach to design is rooted in simplicity and intention. I focus on building strong visual foundations through typography, layout, and hierarchy — creating work that feels both functional and refined.

Through my studies, I have developed skills in HTML and CSS, along with a solid understanding of branding and visual identity. I enjoy taking a concept from early idea stages through to a fully realized design,paying attention to both the big picture and the finer details

I am especially interested in the intersection of design and development — where structure, interaction,and visual language all come together to shape the user experience.

Outside of design, I draw a lot of inspiration from the outdoors. Time spent exploring, observing, and experiencing different environments influences how I think about clarity, balance, and space in my work.

As I continue to grow, I am focused on refining my skills, building meaningful projects, and developing a body of work that reflects both creativity and intention.
